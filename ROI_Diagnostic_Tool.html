What's This Costing You?

Most companies know hiring is broken, but can't quantify the financial impact. This diagnostic calculates your annual cost across four critical areas:

  • Turnover Costs - Replacement, lost productivity, training
  • Bad Hire Impact - Mistakes that had to be corrected
  • Recruiting Inefficiency - Time your managers waste hiring
  • Overtime from Understaffing - The hidden killer
